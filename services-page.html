Prelude music welcomes all guests before the bridal procession and opening prayer.

The ceremony continues with welcome charge, praise and worship, scripture readings, and special music ministration.

Solemnization follows with declaration of intent, exchange of vows and rings, pronouncement, sermon, signing of the register, and presentation of the couple.

Programme Includes

  1. Declaration of Intent
  2. Opening Prayer and Welcome Charge
  3. Praise and Worship
  4. Scripture Readings and Special Music
  5. Exchange of Vows and Rings
  6. Pronouncement and Sermon / Charge
  7. Signing of Register and Recessional
